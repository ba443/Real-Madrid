Our Story

A Legacy Built to Last

1902
Foundation
Real Madrid Club de Fútbol is founded on March 6, 1902 in Madrid, Spain by a group of football enthusiasts.
1920
Royal Patronage
King Alfonso XIII grants the club the title "Real" (Royal), adding the crown to the crest.
1955
European Dominance Begins
Real Madrid wins the inaugural UEFA European Cup, launching an era of continental dominance.
1960
The Legendary Five
Five consecutive European Cups — a record that cemented Madrid as Europe's greatest club of the era.
2002
Centenary Triumph
Real Madrid celebrates its 100th anniversary by winning a ninth Champions League title — La Novena.
2022
La Decimocuarta
Under Carlo Ancelotti, Real Madrid claims its 14th Champions League, defeating Liverpool in Paris.
